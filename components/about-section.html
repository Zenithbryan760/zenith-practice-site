About Zenith Roofing Services, Inc.

Escondido-based roofing team serving San Diego & Temecula

Zenith Roofing Services is a locally owned roofing company built around a simple promise: do the job the right way the first time. From steep-slope homes to low-slope and flat roofs, our crew delivers clean installs, clear communication, and manufacturer-compliant systems designed for Southern California’s heat and seasonal storms.

We specialize in roof replacements, targeted repairs, tile lift & reset, torch down, TPO single-ply, silicone coating systems, and seamless gutters. We pair proven materials (including Owens Corning) with on-site supervision and clean job sites so projects finish on time and without surprises.

  • Assessment first: photo-documented inspection and scope, not guesswork.
  • Clear scope & plan: line-item estimate with options for budget and performance.
  • Execution & QC: code-compliant install, daily cleanup, final walkthrough.
