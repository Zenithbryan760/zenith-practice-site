About Zenith Roofing Services, Inc.

Escondido-based roofing experts serving San Diego & Temecula

We’re a local roofing company headquartered in Escondido, helping homeowners, HOAs, and small businesses across San Diego County and Temecula Valley get dependable roofs that stand up to SoCal sun, wind, and seasonal storms. We use documented install methods and photo updates so you always know what’s being done and why.
