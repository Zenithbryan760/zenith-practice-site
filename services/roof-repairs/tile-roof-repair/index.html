What we repair

  • Broken, slipped, or missing tiles
  • Hairline cracks causing small leaks
  • Minor underlayment exposure at localized areas
  • Counter/step flashing touch-ups at walls, chimneys & skylights
  • Roof penetrations: pipes, vents, satellite mounts (seal & reset)
  • Wind-displaced ridge & rake tiles (re-bed/re-set as needed)

How our service works

  1. Assessment: Inspect the affected areas and confirm the scope.
  2. Source repair: Address the entry point first (tile, flashing, or penetration).
  3. Dry-in: Replace damaged tiles and restore proper laps; seal where appropriate.
  4. Cleanup: Remove debris and leave the work area neat.
  5. Summary: Provide photos and basic maintenance notes.

We focus on targeted leak-source repairs and small sections. For widespread membrane failures or aged underlayment across large areas, we’ll outline practical next steps.

Common questions

Can you match my existing tiles?
When possible we reuse good tiles from the roof and blend with compatible replacements. If an exact color is discontinued, we’ll discuss the closest available option.

Do you perform water testing?
We typically do not perform flood tests on tile systems. Our approach is to locate the likely entry point, make a source repair, and monitor performance.

Will a repair extend the life of my roof?
Localized repairs stop current issues and can buy time; overall service life still depends on the age and condition of the underlayment and flashings.
