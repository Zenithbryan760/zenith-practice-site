Is a Tune-Up Right for My Roof?

Great Fit

  • ✓ No active leaks or isolated, minor leaks
  • ✓ Roof has general wear but isn’t at end-of-life
  • ✓ Annual or pre-rainy season maintenance

Maybe Not

  • ✓ Widespread underlayment/membrane failure
  • ✓ Repeated leaks across multiple planes
  • ✓ Severe deck/structural issues

Shingle, tile, metal, and low-slope (TPO/PVC, torch-down, coatings). Some specialty systems may require a site review first.
