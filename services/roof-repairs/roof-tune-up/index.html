Roof Tune-Up FAQs

How often should I get a roof tune-up?
Typically every 12–24 months, or after major wind/rain events. Commercial roofs with drains benefit from bi-annual service.
Will a tune-up fix active leaks?
Tune-ups are preventative. If we discover an active leak, we’ll perform a targeted repair (with your approval) or schedule it promptly.
Does this extend the life of an older roof?
Yes, within reason. For systems near ~25+ years in SoCal, we’ll show you when replacement becomes the smarter spend.
Residential and commercial?
Yes—single-family homes, HOAs, retail/office, and light industrial. We tailor visit frequency and reporting to your property.
