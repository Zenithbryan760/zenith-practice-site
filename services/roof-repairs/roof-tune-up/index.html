What is a roof tune-up vs. a full repair? +
A tune-up is preventative maintenance: cleaning, sealing, tightening, and small part swaps. If we find damage needing new flashing, underlayment, or larger work, we'll quote that separately.
How often should I schedule roof maintenance? +
Most homes benefit from an annual tune-up; homes under trees, near the coast, or with older roofs may need maintenance every 6‚Äì12 months.
Will a tune-up stop my leak? +
If your leak is caused by a minor issue (like a failed sealant or debris dam), a tune-up may resolve it. If the cause is worn underlayment or flashing failure, we'll recommend a repair.

Yes ‚Äî concrete/clay tile, asphalt shingles, torch-down, and TPO. We tailor maintenance to the roof system and age.
