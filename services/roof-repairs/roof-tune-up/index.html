FAQ

What is a roof tune-up vs. a full repair?
A tune-up is preventative maintenance: cleaning, sealing, tightening, and small part swaps. Larger issues like underlayment or new flashing are quoted separately.
How often should I schedule roof maintenance?
Most homes benefit from an annual tune-up; homes under trees, near the coast, or with older roofs may need maintenance every 6–12 months.
Will a tune-up stop my leak?
If the leak is due to a minor issue such as a failed sealant or debris dam, a tune-up may resolve it. If the cause is worn underlayment or flashing failure, we will recommend a repair.
