Our Roof Tune-Up Process

  1. Inspection & Photos: We walk the roof, note leak-prone areas, and document with pictures.
  2. Maintenance & Minor Repairs: Debris removal, sealing/caulking, fastener work, and small part swaps where feasible.
  3. Clean-Up & Report: We tidy up and provide simple notes with recommendations.
  4. Optional Repairs: If we find issues beyond tune-up scope, we’ll provide a separate estimate.

Roof Tune-Up FAQs

Will this stop my leak?
A tune-up often fixes minor causes (cracked sealant, debris dams). If the source is worn underlayment or failed flashing, we’ll quote a repair.
How often should I schedule maintenance?
Annually for most homes; every 6–12 months if under trees, near the coast, or with older roofs.
What roof types do you service?
Concrete/clay tile, asphalt shingles, torch-down, and TPO.
