What’s Included

Straightforward maintenance designed to prevent leaks and improve drainage. Scope may vary by system and condition.

  • Replace up to 5 concrete tiles (not clay) or up to 5 shingles when matching stock is available.
  • Seal and secure vent pipes/boots; touch‑up flashing paint to help prevent rust.
  • Clear valleys and clean ~100 ft of gutters (two‑story may require additional safety/access).
  • Cover exposed nails at ridge/caps and minor nail pop‑outs (abnormal perimeter nail lines/heavy caulking quoted separately).
