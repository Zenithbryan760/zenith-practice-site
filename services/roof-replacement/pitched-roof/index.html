What counts as a “pitched” roof?

A pitched (sloped) roof is a water-shedding roof—gravity moves water off quickly. If your roof is ≳ 3:12, you’re in the pitched category.

Is asphalt shingle the same as architectural/composition/three-tab?
Yes — different names for similar products. We’ll match the right profile and warranty for your home.
Can I switch materials (e.g., tile → shingles or shingles → metal)?
Often yes; we’ll verify structure/weight/ventilation/code and HOA before recommending changes.
How long does replacement take?
Most homes: 1–4 days depending on size, layers, repairs, and weather.
Do you handle permits and clean-up?
Yes. We pull permits as required, protect landscaping, magnet sweep nails, and haul debris.
