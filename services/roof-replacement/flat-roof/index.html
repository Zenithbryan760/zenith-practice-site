Flat Roof FAQs

TPO vs PVC — how do I choose?

Both are single-ply with heat-welded seams. PVC excels near grease/chemicals; TPO is widely used for energy savings.

Recover or full tear-off?

We verify code and deck condition. If saturated or over the layer limit, tear-off is required.
