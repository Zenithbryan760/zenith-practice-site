How to tell in under a minute

  1. Stand back in the driveway or across the street. If you see a clear slope, choose Pitched. If it looks flat or nearly flat, choose Flat.
  2. Look at the surface: small overlapping pieces = shingles; curved or “S” shapes = tile; smooth or white sheet-like surface = flat membrane (TPO/PVC) or torch down.
  3. Still unsure? Tap “I’m not sure — please take a look” and we’ll handle it.
