Our Process

1) Intake & Triage

We review your photos, claim number, and carrier notes. If active leaks exist, we prioritize temporary dry-in.

2) Site Visit & Documentation

On-site assessment, photos, and condition notes. We identify storm-related damages and code items.

3) Scope Review

We compare repair/replacement options against the carrier scope and align line items, quantities, and materials.

4) Proposal & Next Steps

Clear written scope with scheduling and warranty notes. We can coordinate with your adjuster if needed.

FAQs

Can you meet my adjuster on site?

Yes. Joint inspections help align scope and answer technical questions quickly. Tell us your adjuster’s availability.

Is every storm mark a replacement?

Not always. Some issues are repairable; others need system replacement. We document conditions so decisions are defensible.
