What’s Free vs. Paid?

For homeowners asking us to inspect their own roof for normal wear/tear or to price a repair, our inspection is free. For third-party requests (insurance, lender/refi, HOA, property manager, or letters), a $295 letter applies, and a $495 certified report is available when you need formal documentation with photos.

Real-Estate FAQ

How fast can you inspect and deliver?

We typically schedule within a few business days and deliver most visual inspections and estimates by the next business day after the visit.

What if a certification isn’t eligible?

We’ll provide a deficiency list and a path to eligibility. After repairs, we can re-inspect and issue the certificate if standards are met.

Do you handle storm-damage insurance inspections?

Yes—storm damage claim inspections are free for active claims. For other third-party requests, see our $295 letter and $495 certified report options above.

Can you give a quick opinion before I order?

Yes—our Verbal Roof Opinion is free. We’ll help you choose the right deliverable for your situation.
