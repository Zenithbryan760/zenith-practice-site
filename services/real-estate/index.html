What’s Free vs. Paid for Inspections?

For homeowners asking us to inspect their own roof for normal wear/tear or to price a repair, our homeowner inspection is free. For third-party requests (insurance, lender/refi, HOA, property manager, or formal letters), choose a $295 letter or a $495 certified report depending on what your counterpart requires.

Real-Estate FAQ

How fast can you inspect and deliver?

We prioritize transaction timelines: typically schedule within a few business days and deliver visual inspections/estimates by the next business day after the visit.

What if a roof certification isn’t eligible?

We provide a deficiency list and a path to eligibility. After required fixes, we can re-inspect and issue the certificate when standards are met.

Do you handle storm-damage insurance inspections?

Yes—storm-damage claim inspections are free for active claims. Otherwise use our $295 letter or $495 certified report.

Can I get quick guidance before ordering?

Absolutely—our Verbal Roof Opinion is free by phone to help you choose the right service.
