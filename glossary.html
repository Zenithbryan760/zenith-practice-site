Roofing Glossary

Underlayment
A waterproof or water-resistant layer under the shingles to protect your roof deck.
Modified Bitumen
A torch-down or self-adhesive flat roof material reinforced for durability.
Dead Valley
Low-slope areas of a roof where water collects and must be specially waterproofed.
