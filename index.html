Roofing Glossary

An A–Z reference of common roofing terms so you can talk the talk and make informed decisions.

Asphalt Shingle
A widely used roofing material composed of a fiberglass or organic mat coated with asphalt and topped with mineral granules for UV protection.
Base Sheet
The first ply of roofing membrane in a built‑up or modified‑bitumen roof system, typically fastened directly to the deck.
Cool Roof (Title 24)
A roofing surface designed to reflect more sunlight and absorb less heat, required by California’s energy code in certain climate zones.
Dead Valley
A section of roof with minimal slope where water can collect; often requires special waterproofing like torch‑down or tapered insulation.
Modified Bitumen
A durable, asphalt‑based roofing membrane reinforced with modifiers (APP or SBS) and installed via torch, cold‑adhesive, or self‑adhesive methods.
Underlayment
A water‑resistant or waterproof barrier installed under the primary roof covering to protect the deck from moisture.
